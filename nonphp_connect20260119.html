Getting Started

Your first steps in 3Kingdoms

Creating Your Character

Once connected, you'll be greeted with a login screen. Press Enter to continue, then you'll see options to either log in with an existing character or create a new one.

New players: When prompted for your character name, choose something unique and creative. Names cannot contain numbers or profanity. If the name is already taken, you'll be asked for a password - just disconnect and try a different name.

You can also login as guest to explore the game before creating a character.

The Tutorial

After creating your character, you'll be offered a basic tutorial. We strongly recommend taking it if you're new to MUDs or 3Kingdoms. It will teach you fundamental commands and help you navigate your first steps.

The tutorial is interactive and will guide you through movement, combat basics, and essential commands to get you started on your adventure.

Getting Help

3Kingdoms has an extensive support system for new players:

  • Newbie Helpers: Dedicated players who volunteer their time to help newcomers. Use the newb channel to ask questions.
  • Help System: Type help anytime for access to comprehensive help files.
  • Newbie Area: A dedicated safe zone designed for learning the basics.
  • Community: The player community is welcoming and helpful - don't hesitate to ask!

Example: newb Hi! I'm new, can someone help me?

Why Use a Client?

While the web client is perfect for getting started, dedicated MUD clients offer features that will significantly enhance your experience:

  • Triggers: Automatic responses to game events
  • Aliases: Shortcut commands for complex actions
  • Macros: Bind actions to function keys
  • Mappers: Visual maps of your location
  • Sound Packs: Audio enhancement (Portal™)
  • Scrollback: Review previous text easily
  • Color Customization: Personalize your display
  • And much more!
